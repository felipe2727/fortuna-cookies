# New Image Generation Prompts for "fortuna." Product Detail

The previous images had watermarks, alignment issues, and were framed too tightly with dark backgrounds making them hard to use in an editorial layout. We are pivoting to a "Biologica" inspired layout.

For this new high-end, brutalist/editorial layout to work, **the images need to be extremely high quality, fully isolated subject matter, with plenty of negative space around them so they can scale.**

Please run these prompts and drop them back in the `public` folder when ready.

---

### 1. The Main Product Focus (Save as: `public/main-product.jpg`)
**Prompt:**
> "A single, incredibly sleek, matte black metallic tin box. The box has a highly reflective, subtle futuristic silver sheen. High-end luxury e-commerce product photography, 8k resolution, photorealistic, **shot on a perfectly solid, seamless, stark white or light gray background.** The box must be perfectly centered with a large amount of empty negative space around it."

*This will be the massive hero image on the left side of the split screen.*

### 2. The Ingredients Focus (Save as: `public/ingredients.jpg`)
